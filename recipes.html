Welcome to Panlasang Pinay

Panlasang Pinay is a website dedicated to showcasing the rich and diverse culinary heritage of the Philippines. We share authentic Filipino recipes, cooking tips, and food culture for everyone—from home cooks to seasoned chefs.

From classic adobo and sinigang to modern Filipino-inspired dishes, our goal is to inspire your cooking journey.

Top Recipes

1. Chicken Empanada

Chicken Empanadas are a simple yet filling and delicious turnover pastry, and despite their compact half moon size are meals in themselves. No pretense necessary –– wonderful and juicy, empanadas are the no-frill snack everyone will love.

2. Siopao Adobo

Siopao Adobo is a Filipino steamed bun filled with savory adobo meat. The soft, fluffy bun encases tender, flavorful pork or chicken adobo, making it a delicious and convenient snack or meal. Perfect for on-the-go enjoyment!

3. Embutido

Embutido is a traditional Filipino sausage made from ground meat, usually pork, mixed with various spices and seasonings. It is often served sliced and accompanied by rice or bread.

4. Pandesal

Pandesal is a traditional Filipino soft bread that is often served with butter or as a side to meals. It is light, fluffy, and slightly sweet, making it a popular breakfast item.

5. Lumpiang Sariwa

Lumpiang Sariwa is a Filipino spring roll made with fresh vegetables and wrapped in a thin, crispy wrapper. It is often served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.
